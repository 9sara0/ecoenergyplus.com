About Us

Eco Energy provides power solutions and consultancy that will help you reduce your electricity bill

Our Mission

We aim to cut down our costumer’s electricity bill who are not covered by governmental support by providing premium consultancy and implementation of energy audit and saving measures. We will also upgrade their facility to be environmentally friendly

Our Vision

To save money, energy and preserve environment

Our Values

WE support sustainable development in line with Bahrain 2030 vision, we:

• Reduce carbon foot print to support local and global environment.

• Integrate sustainable sources within the built environment.

• Contribute in achieving goals set by the vision.

We also Support local businesses, real estate owners and non-Bahrainis living in Bahrain by:

• Contributing in cutting operational expenses.

• Taking financial load off of the local businesses and their customers by coming in early in the production chain to minimize the total cost.

Our Services

Site visits in order to check the facility
Perform building audit (level 1 & Level 2)
Produce the analysis report that indicates the flaws in the building with the required measures
Apply the recommended measures
Follow-up with the client to ensure the process
Provide architectural consultation (Pre-build)
